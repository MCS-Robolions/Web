WHAT IS FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a worldwide organization sponsoring robotics competitions for students. It was created in 1989 to promote interest in science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Students learn to perform high-quality, challenging work in a competitive but collaborative environment. There is, however, more to FIRST than building robots. During FIRST competitions, teams are rewarded for excellence in design, demonstrated team spirit, Gracious Professionalism® and the ability to overcome obstacles.

WHY ROBOTICS?

FIRST Robotics is all about having fun while learning, and so are we! Along the way our team members learn technical skills from mechanical and electrical engineering, computer programming, marketing and public relations, but most importantly, they learn about teamwork, leadership, respect and integrity.
