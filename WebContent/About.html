About

The Maryville Christian High School (MCHS) Robotics Team, the RoboLions, exists to provide students with a collaborative environment and experiences to encourage interest in STEM fields. Our goals include reaching out to the community and fostering appreciation in math and science. At the core of this effort is to develop young men and women with hands-on, real world engineering and business experience who are independent critical thinkers with a Christian worldview.

This is the RoboLions rookie year as a FIRST® FTC Team. We are excited to begin building, programming and competing. Because this is the inaugural year for our team the focus will be on robot development and community partnership.
